What is Audit Army?

🎖️

Like Military Badges

Just as army veterans earn badges for their achievements and service, businesses earn verification badges through rigorous auditing. Each DAO awards their own custom badges - security cleared, code reviewed, finances verified, food safety certified, and more.

🏛️

Create Your Own BBB

Think of us as "WordPress for Better Business Bureaus." Anyone can launch a specialized Review DAO - crypto audits, restaurant inspections, healthcare compliance, financial services verification. Each DAO sets its own standards and specializes in its niche.

💰

DAO Revenue Model

Every audit conducted through your DAO earns 20% for your treasury. Your reviewers earn 80% for quality work. Build a sustainable business model while providing value to your community.

🛡️

Smart Contract Protection

All payments are securely held in escrow until the audit is complete and approved by the DAO community. If the review doesn't meet standards, businesses get a full refund. Trust without risk.

⚖️

Governance & Voting

Each DAO is self-governing. Members vote on reviewer quality, audit standards, and whether reviews pass. Your community decides what quality means in your industry.

🎯

Specialized Expertise

Instead of one-size-fits-all ratings, you get niche experts. Crypto auditors know smart contracts. Healthcare reviewers understand regulations. Restaurant inspectors know food safety. Each DAO builds deep expertise.
